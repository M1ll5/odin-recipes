Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups shortening
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1tbs distilled white vinegar
  • 4 tbs water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix flour and salt. Cut in shortening with two butter knives.
  2. In seperate bowl, mix together egg, vinegar and water. Drizzel wet mixture into dry mixture, cutting it in.
  3. Roll out dough, and fit into two 9 inch pie pans.
  4. Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 12 minutes.
